ZEEN

ZEEN* started out among friends who shared a passion for creativity. We hope this publication can serve as a platform where our works can be shared through our means.

Why the asterisk in our name? It represents many things we cannot say. The idea of * has always been utilized to call things to attention, point to what is omitted, or to correct. It aligns with the new, but acknowledges what came before.

It gives a voice to the unknown.
