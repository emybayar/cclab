ISSUE 01

SPRING 23

In this first issue you will find all sorts of creative writing, art, photography, along with special interviews. We explore the theme of “elasticity”—rather than change. The identity does not transform into something completely different, but stretches itself into something greater and larger, or the other way around, pulling inward.

This issue features POSTMODERNIST by NARTAY UALIKHAN; BAIJIU CONFESSIONS; SHANGHAI NIGHTLIFE; TRENDS, TATTOO TOUR by ANNIE CHURCH; LATCHKEY by TIFFANY LEONG; I HOPE YOU KNOW I LOVE YOU by MAYLEE SEXTON; interviews with AMINA ALMATOVA, NUR AKSAMIJA, KEIGAN CARPENTER; A DESCENT INTO ALL-ENCOMPASSING SADNESS by EMY SAINBAYAR; SOMETHING I DID IN HIGH SCHOOL by SOPHIA TUREK. 5.8 x 8.3’’ COLOR, 40 pages explore spreads one and two to get a sneak peek, as well as behind-the-scenes from issue01 explore the cover for a surprise!
